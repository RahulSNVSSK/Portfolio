Data Management Hub – Analytical MDM

UK Water Utilities Client | June 2023 – Present

This project focuses on building an Analytical Master Data Management (MDM) solution for Property, Asset, and Customer master domains as part of a strategic data governance initiative. Data from multiple enterprise systems including Reference Data sets, AI2, SAP S/4HANA, ABP, GIS, SAP IS-U, and SAP ECC are integrated into the Profisee MDM platform.

Azure Data Factory (ADF) is used to ingest data via REST APIs, after which data quality validation rules are applied to ensure accuracy and consistency. Matching and survivorship strategies are implemented to generate reliable golden records, establishing a centralized single source of truth within Profisee. The mastered data is then published to Data Lake storage, where Power BI dashboards are built to compare inbound and outbound data quality metrics. This solution supports enhanced data governance, quality monitoring, and analytics enablement for a UK-based water utilities organization.


Profisee MDM Integration with AWS Glue & Azure ADF – PoC

Hexaware Technologies | March 2023 – May 2023

This Proof of Concept (PoC) was designed to demonstrate the feasibility of integrating the Profisee MDM platform with AWS Glue and Azure Data Factory. The focus was on streamlining data ingestion, processing, and golden record generation using cloud-native ETL services.

REST APIs were leveraged to enable seamless communication between Profisee, AWS Glue, and Azure ADF. The PoC showcased efficient data movement, transformation, and master data consolidation, highlighting the flexibility of Profisee in hybrid cloud integration scenarios and supporting enterprise-scale data management use cases.


Profisee RDM & MDM Center of Excellence (CoE) – Pre-Sales

Hexaware Technologies | November 2022 – February 2023

This initiative focused on building an end-to-end Reference Data Management (RDM) and Master Data Management (MDM) solution within the Profisee Center of Excellence to support pre-sales activities. The solution was designed to demonstrate the full capabilities of the Profisee platform to prospective clients across various industries.

The implementation included configurable RDM and MDM models, governance workflows, and data quality rules, along with Power BI dashboard integration for visualization and reporting. This pre-sales solution served as a customizable demonstration framework, enabling clients to understand how Profisee can address their specific data governance and master data requirements.
